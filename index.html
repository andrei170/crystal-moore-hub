At 1-2% conversion on a warmed list, that is 36-144 buyer or seller inquiries never had. At average South Florida commission, the pipeline miss over 12 months is material.

Fast Install
02

No Digital Nurture Sequence After First Contact

The only path to Crystal today is: see IG content, go to dominionmoves.com, call or fill the contact form. There is no email sequence. No pre-call education that arrives before the consultation. No post-consult follow-up drip. Single-channel intake on a broker who closes $100M+ is a structural fragility. Crystal's CRB credential, 13 years of market knowledge, and SFR designation are not packaged into any automated touchpoint that runs without her presence. Redfin, Zillow-affiliated teams, and RE/MAX have full CRM drip running around the clock. Crystal competes on relationship. She is not yet compounding the relationship digitally.

Cost of Waiting

Each buyer or seller who reaches out, gets a manual follow-up or none, and goes cold is a $8K-$18K commission left in the pipeline. At 10% lead-to-close rate, every 10 stale leads equals one missed closing. If 20% of consultations go cold after first contact with no follow-up drip, that is 10 missed closings per year of commissions a nurture sequence would have recovered.

6 Months From NowAnother 6 months of post-consultation leads going cold. Zero infrastructure to recover them.

Fix: 5-7 email post-inquiry sequence. Day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30. South Florida market insight, insurance math, buyer pre-qualification checklist, investor ROI framework. Ends with a calendar link. Runs on autopilot in GHL or ConvertKit.
Compound Pickup
03

Agent Recruitment Has No Funnel Behind the Calendly Link

dominionmoves.com advertises "Join South Florida's Top Boutique Brokerage - 100% Commission Plans Available." There is a Calendly link. That is the entire recruitment funnel. No landing page built for agent acquisition. No VSL from Crystal explaining the Dominion culture and what 100% commission actually means for a producing agent. No email sequence for agents considering a switch. Agent recruitment is a B2B sale with a longer cycle than a consumer transaction. The agents Crystal wants to recruit are comparing Dominion against brokerages with full pitch decks, culture videos, and onboarding documents. The information gap makes the 100% commission plan look unsubstantiated.

Cost of Waiting

Each agent who joins Dominion represents desk fees and transaction revenue on every deal they close. One productive agent closing $3M-$5M per year equals $15K-$25K per year in brokerage revenue. Every month with no agent funnel equals zero systematic recruitment activity. Over 12 months with even 2 recruitable agents missed: $30K-$50K in recurring brokerage revenue foregone.

6 Months From NowAnother 6 months of qualified agents evaluating brokerages without a Dominion-specific conversion path.

Fix: Dedicated agent recruitment landing page with Crystal's VSL ("Why I Built Dominion and What 100% Commission Actually Means"), a 3-email agent nurture sequence, and a simple agent testimonial block. Install in 4-5 days post quiz launch.
