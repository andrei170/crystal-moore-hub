Target audience: Buyers, sellers, and investors in Coral Springs, Boca Raton, Parkland, Plantation, Pompano Beach.

Format: Talking head. Crystal on camera. 3-4 minutes.

Outline

Hook (0:00-0:20) "The South Florida real estate market shifted in 2024 -- and most buyers and sellers I talk to are working off outdated assumptions. I am Crystal Marie Moore. I have closed over $100 million in South Florida real estate over 13 years. In the next four minutes, I will show you the three things that are actually moving the market right now and why most of what you read online is behind the curve."

Problem identification (0:20-1:00) "If you are a buyer in Coral Springs or Boca Raton right now, you are probably running the numbers and wondering why the homes you can afford today are different from what you could afford 18 months ago. The answer is not just interest rates. It is homeowner insurance. In some South Florida zip codes, insurance premiums have increased 40-60% since 2022. That is an expense most buyers are not factoring into their monthly payment until we sit down and run the real numbers."

Authority establishment (1:00-1:30) "I hold three designations most Florida brokers do not: CRB -- the highest credential for brokerage management -- and SFR and MRP, which cover short sales, foreclosures, and military relocations specifically. I have been in this market for 13 years. I founded Dominion Realty Group in 2016 because I wanted to deliver the level of personal care that large brokerages cannot. Over $100 million in closed sales. More than 1,000 clients served."

Market intel (1:30-2:30) "Here is what the data shows right now in our primary markets: [insert Crystal's current market stats on inventory, days on market, price changes by submarket]. The condo market specifically requires a different conversation. HB 913 and SB 4-D changed what buyers need to know before putting in an offer on any pre-2000 condo building. If your agent is not explaining the milestone inspection and reserve funding requirements, you may be buying a liability, not an asset."

Solution bridge (2:30-3:15) "What I do differently: preparation. Every consultation I run, I come to you with current comps, current insurance data for the specific property, HOA financials if it is a condo, and a complete breakdown of your actual monthly cost including taxes, insurance, and HOA fees. My clients do not get surprised after closing. They get the full picture at the first meeting."

Call to action (3:15-3:45) "If you are thinking about buying, selling, or investing in South Florida in the next 6-12 months, schedule a no-cost consultation through the link below. I want to look at your specific situation and tell you what the market looks like for your price range and your timeline. The calendar link is right below this video."

Sign-off (3:45-4:00) "I am Crystal Marie Moore. Your vision meets our excellence."
